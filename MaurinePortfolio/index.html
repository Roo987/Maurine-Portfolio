About me

Hey there! I'm a fourth-year student studying Business Information Technology. I love everything about tech, especially making websites. I've been learning Java, C++, Python, HTML, CSS, PHP and JavaScript for the past four years.

I think making websites is like building digital art. I've practiced a lot, making sure websites look good and are easy to use. I believe websites should work for everyone, so I focus on making them accessible and engaging.

Apart from studying, I enjoy being part of the tech community. I go to workshops and join hackathons to learn and work on cool projects. I'm excited to keep learning and using my skills to create awesome stuff on the web!

In my journey as a BBIT student, I've had the opportunity to apply my knowledge and skills in real-world scenarios, collaborating with classmates and clients alike to solve challenges and deliver impactful solutions. Through these experiences, I've gained valuable insights into project management, teamwork, and communication, further enriching my professional development.

Web Design

I create beautiful and user-friendly website designs tailored to your needs and preferences. Whether you're starting from scratch or need a redesign, I'll work closely with you to bring your vision to life.

Web Development

I specialize in turning designs into functional websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. From simple static sites to dynamic web applications, I'll build a website that meets your requirements and exceeds your expectations.

E-Commerce Solutions

I specialize in developing e-commerce websites that enable businesses to sell products or services online. From setting up product listings and secure payment gateways to implementing shopping carts and order management systems, I'll create a seamless e-commerce experience for your customers.

Responsive Design

I ensure your website looks great and works seamlessly across all devices, from desktops to smartphones. With responsive design techniques, I'll make sure your content adapts to different screen sizes, providing a consistent user experience.
