Ongoing project

McDonald Consultancy Booking system

McDonald is a physiotherapy clinic located at South C. The project showcased is still a work in progress The project has the home page which showcases the various sections in the website. It has the services section which shows what the hospital offers. It allows users(patients) to create their own accounts. They fill in a triage form with their details.

They can also book apppointments at a time that is suitable for them and with the doctor of their choice. If the specific physiotherapist is not available they will be given recommendations on other doctors, and also the free slots available for the physiotherapists. Both the patients and physiotherapists receive reminders on booked appointments.

The website also has a contact section which allows the patients to give feedback on the services received and also ask any questions they may have.
